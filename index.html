Projects/Publications

Ongoing and Completed

Some of these below mentioned projects are completed and some are ongoing. This list contains the special ones. For publications follow this link.

Project

Neural Jitter and Shimmer

Neural Jitter and Shimmer

Neural correlate of Jitter and Shimmer parameters are used for further processing of eeg data and extraction of properties.

Project

Gestalt Phenomenon in Music

Gestalt Phenomenon in Music

We try to find where the recognition of music is not possible by using different frequency bands of same music involving both brain and human responses.

Project

Effect of Music on Plants

Effect of Music on Plants

Following the footsteps of J. C. Bose we attempted in looking the effect of music in plants, by seeing their growth rates. This is an extremely exciting experiment.

Project

Musical Meditation

Musical Meditation

What is Music? Listening to Music is source of recreation, a favourite pastime for many. It soothes the stress, tranquilizes our soul. In order to become a healthy human being Music can be an essential medicine with a superb healing power. Meditation is a practice which induces a mode of consciousness. This can be done with Music as well. Hence our venture.

Project

Sonification

Sonification

We look forward to sonify eeg data of low frequency to a higher frequency audible data in this project.

Project

Void Estimation

Void Estimation

In this project we will estimate the void present in a music signal and use it as a measure for identification of different styles of music and also for singer identification.
